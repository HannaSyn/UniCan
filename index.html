About Us

OVERVIEW

UniCan provides innovative, global supply-chain solutions across Consumer, Industrial and Retail markets. These solutions include Order Management, Distribution & Fulfillment, Customs Brokerage and Transportation products delivered across Canada.

With roots in ocean shipping, our brand was built alongside a culture of deep customer focus and attention. One of our advantages is that we find and conclude exclusive contracts for the import of products. Today, the portfolio of the company has an extremely wide assortment - canned food, vegetable oil, frozen bread, cereals, textiles.

Mission

Our company works with the “most” leading, “most” qualified and “most” professional factories of the industry and it provides service in terms of exporting products to overseas markets.
